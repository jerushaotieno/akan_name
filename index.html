MY AKAN NAME

What's in a name? If you were born in Ghana, your first name would be a "day name" corresponding to the day of the week you were born and your gender.

My Akan Name is a web application allowing you to calculate the day of the week you were born depending on your birthday and gender after which it gives you your corresponding Ghanaian Akan Name for a taste of Ghanaian naming culture.

To find out what your Ghanaian name would be, fill in the form below. Have fun!
